# Social Cognition

## Definition
How people process, store, and apply information about other people and social situations.

## Key Processes
- Theory of mind / mentalizing
- Person perception and impression formation
- Social inference and attribution
- Norms, roles, and group processes
- Empathy and perspective-taking

## Related Constructs
Identity (social identity), emotion (contagion), belief (stereotypes), behavior (social influence), metacognition (social meta-cognition).

## Research Notes
Social identity theory, attribution theory, theory of mind development, mirror neurons / simulation accounts, intergroup bias research.

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Useful for collaboration agents and multi-user canon sharing.
- Pattern analyst: surface social dynamics in team or community notes.
- Identity module integration for role and group identities.
- Future support for agent swarms modeling social learning.
