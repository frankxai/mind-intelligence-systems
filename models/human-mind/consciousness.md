# Consciousness

## Definition
The subjective experience of being aware, including phenomenal experience, access consciousness, and self-awareness.

## Key Processes
- Phenomenal consciousness (what it is like)
- Access consciousness (reportable content)
- Self-consciousness / meta-awareness
- States of consciousness (wake, sleep, flow, etc.)

## Related Constructs
Attention, metacognition, emotion (affective consciousness), identity.

## Research Notes
Hard problem (Chalmers), global workspace theory (Baars/Dehaene), integrated information theory, predictive processing views.

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Used sparingly; focus on meta-awareness in reflection and review skills.
- Support journaling of phenomenal reports without interpretation.
- Link to metacognition module for monitoring.
