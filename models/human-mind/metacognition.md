# Metacognition

## Definition
Cognition about cognition — monitoring, control, and knowledge of one's own mental processes.

## Key Processes
- Monitoring (feeling of knowing, confidence)
- Control (planning, strategy selection, termination)
- Metamemory, meta-attention, meta-learning

## Related Constructs
Learning, attention, memory, consciousness, decision-making.

## Research Notes
Flavell foundational work, Nelson & Narens model, monitoring-control framework, desirable difficulties.

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Core to weekly review and personal canon building.
- Active recall generator includes confidence judgments.
- Learning architect designs for calibration of confidence.
- Pattern analyst surfaces metacognitive illusions (overconfidence, etc.).
