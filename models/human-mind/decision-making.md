# Decision-Making

## Definition
The processes by which individuals select among alternatives, form intentions, and commit to courses of action.

## Key Processes
- Option generation and evaluation
- Value and utility assessment
- Risk and uncertainty handling
- Implementation intentions and planning
- Post-decision evaluation and regret

## Related Constructs
Motivation, belief, emotion (somatic markers), metacognition (confidence), behavior.

## Research Notes
Expected utility, prospect theory (Kahneman & Tversky), dual-process (System 1/2), implementation intentions (Gollwitzer), ecological rationality.

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Pattern analyst: detect decision patterns and biases in notes.
- Learning architect: design choice architectures for better learning decisions.
- Weekly review: surface key decisions and their outcomes for reflection.
- Link to research claim graphs for evidence-based decisions.
