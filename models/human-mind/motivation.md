# Motivation

## Definition
The processes that initiate, guide, and maintain goal-directed behavior.

## Key Processes
- Intrinsic vs. extrinsic motivation
- Approach / avoidance
- Goal setting and commitment
- Expectancy and value (expectancy-value theory)
- Self-determination (autonomy, competence, relatedness)

## Related Constructs
Emotion, attention (selective to goal-relevant), learning (reinforcement), behavior, identity (possible selves).

## Research Notes
Drive theory, incentive motivation, self-determination theory (Deci & Ryan), goal systems (Kruglanski).

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Daily capture: capture current goals and energy/approach vs avoidance.
- Pattern analyst: link motivation patterns to output and learning streaks.
- Learning architect: design workflows that support autonomy and competence.
