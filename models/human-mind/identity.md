# Identity

## Definition
The sense of self, including roles, traits, narratives, and possible selves that organize experience and guide behavior.

## Key Processes
- Self-concept maintenance and updating
- Narrative identity (McAdams)
- Social identity and role identities
- Possible selves and future self-continuity

## Related Constructs
Memory (autobiographical), belief (core beliefs about self), behavior (identity-behavior consistency), metacognition (self-reflection).

## Research Notes
Erikson stages, social identity theory (Tajfel), narrative identity, self-discrepancy theory.

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Identity vault section: capture current roles, values, future selves.
- Personal canon builder: surface recurring identity themes from notes.
- Weekly review: reflect on identity-consistent vs inconsistent actions.
