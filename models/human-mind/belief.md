# Belief

## Definition
Mental representations held to be true about the world, self, and others that guide interpretation and action.

## Key Processes
- Belief formation and updating (Bayesian)
- Confirmation bias and belief perseverance
- Epistemic vs. instrumental beliefs
- Core beliefs vs. peripheral

## Related Constructs
Learning, identity, emotion (beliefs shape appraisal), decision-making, behavior.

## Research Notes
Cognitive dissonance (Festinger), belief bias in reasoning, predictive processing as belief updating.

## Agent Prompts / Usage
- Pattern analyst: surface conflicting or unexamined beliefs in notes.
- Source distillation: separate claims, evidence, and interpretations.
- Memory governor: tag source reliability and belief strength.
- Claim graph workflows in research repos.
